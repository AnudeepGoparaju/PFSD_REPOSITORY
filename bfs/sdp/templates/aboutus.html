About Us

Local Bank of India is India's largest bank with a network of over 25,000 branches located even in the remotest parts of India. We offer a wide range of banking products and services to corporate and retail customers.

This is the Internet banking portal for Local Bank of India. The portal provides anywhere, anytime, online access to accounts for Local Bank's Retail and Corporate customers. The application is developed using the latest cutting-edge technology and tools. The infrastructure supports unified, secure access to banking services for accounts in over 25,000 branches across India.

The Retail banking application is an integration of several functional areas and enables customers to:

  • Issue Demand Drafts online
  • Transfer funds to own and third-party accounts
  • Generate account statements
  • Avail DEMAT and IPO services
